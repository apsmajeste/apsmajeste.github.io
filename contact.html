Quick answers

Before you write

What are your business hours?
Our team is available Monday through Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST. Messages received outside these hours are answered the next business day.
Can I book a fragrance consultation?
Yes. We offer complimentary 30-minute consultations by phone or video with one of our fragrance specialists. Mention "fragrance consultation" in the subject line and we will share three available time slots.
How can I become a distributor or dealer?
We welcome enquiries from distributors, retailers, modern trade partners, and corporate buyers across India. Please submit your firm name, contact person name and number, and city & state with pincode via our contact form or email us at office@apsmajeste.com.
Where is APS Majeste available?
APS Majeste products are available on Amazon, Myntra, JioMart, and Flipkart. We also have a growing distributor and dealer network across Ahmedabad, Udaipur, Bhilwara, Chittorgarh, and Rajsamand.
