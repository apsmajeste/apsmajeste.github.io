A quiet brand, built on a simple belief

Skincare should be slow, intentional, and made well — by people you could know, with botanicals you could trace. APS MAJESTE was founded to make that belief tangible.

The Beginning

From an Indian field to a Udaipur atelier

The founder grew up between Kannauj and the Aravalli hills, where her grandmother distilled rose water each spring in a copper still that still sits in our atelier. After a decade formulating for Indian perfume houses, she returned to Udaipur in 2018 with a single question: what would skincare look like if we treated every ingredient the way her grandmother treated her roses — patiently, by hand, and only at the right moment?

The answer became APS MAJESTE. Launched in 2019 with a small line of face washes and perfumes and a single grower, the brand now works with twelve Indian farms, two distillers, and a small team of chemists in Udaipur — but the principles have not changed. We harvest at peak potency. We blend in small batches. We test relentlessly. And we tell the truth about what each formula can and cannot do.

Today, APS MAJESTE is carried in every state, but every bottle still passes through the hands of a person who signed it.

Our Mission

To make skincare that respects the people who make it, the people who use it, and the land it comes from.

We are a B-Corp certified brand committed to fair wages for our growers, transparent ingredient sourcing, and formulas that perform without greenwashing. Every product page lists full INCI. Every batch is signed. Every promise is kept.
